Wispr Clone

Voice typing for your browser.

1. Enable the microphone (one time)

Click below and choose Allow. This grants mic access to the extension once, so you won't be asked on every website.

2. How to use

  1. Click into any text box on a web page (Gmail, X, ChatGPT, a search bar…).
  2. Press ⌘⇧Y (or click the toolbar icon → Start).
  3. Speak. Your words are typed in. Press the shortcut again to stop.

Change the shortcut

Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts to customize the dictation hotkey.

Note: in-browser dictation uses the browser's built-in speech engine (Google), so audio is processed in the cloud. For fully on-device, private dictation, use the Wispr Clone Mac app.
